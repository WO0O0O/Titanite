9. **Cloud & Operations (Layer O) Moat Audit:** Not applicable as NRGV is a physical hardware and software integrator, not a GPU cloud provider.

Energy Vault acts as a systems integrator in a highly competitive, low-margin BESS market. The company has failed to establish a proprietary gravity storage footprint with hyperscalers and lacks the sole-source design wins required for chokepoint status.
